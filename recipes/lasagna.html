Easy Vegetable Lasagne Recipe







Description

Today we present you an easy Lasagna recipe thats optimal for Summer.

It just takes a few simple Ingedrients and is vegetarian. Recipe is for 4 People.


Ingredients

  • ~10 Lasagna Sheets
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 300gr spinach
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • some pine nuts
  • olive oil
  • salt & pepper
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna a little over halfway through
  2. In the meantime cook the onions with some olive oil in the pan and add tomatoes after they`re blanched
  3. Add the spinach into the sauce and let it cook for 5 min
  4. Add salt & pepper to taste
  5. Assemble your lasagna into a glass bowl start with sauce then a few pine nuts and the sheets
  6. Top the Lasagna with the sauce, pine nuts and the Parmesan cheese
  7. Put it in the Oven for 15 min at 220Â° Celsius


Enjoy!
